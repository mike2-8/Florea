The Basic Care treatment is a gentle yet effective skincare ritual designed to cleanse, nourish, and restore your skin's natural radiance. Using premium botanical ingredients, this signature treatment targets dullness, uneven texture, and early signs of dehydration — leaving your skin visibly softer, brighter, and deeply refreshed.

Ideal for first-time guests or those seeking a calming everyday treatment, Basic Care is suitable for all skin types including sensitive skin. No harsh chemicals, no artificial fragrances — just pure, mindful skincare the way nature intended.

Step 1 Basic Skin Consultation — A brief skin assessment to understand your skin type, concerns, and sensitivities before the treatment begins (5 min)
Step 2 Gentle Facial Cleansing — Remove impurities, excess oil, and makeup residue using a mild botanical cleanser suited for all skin types (10 min)
Step 3 Steam Therapy — Open pores and soften the skin surface to prepare for deeper product absorption (5 min)
Step 4 Light Hydration Treatment — Infuse the skin with a nourishing essence and hyaluronic-based serum to restore moisture balance and improve skin texture (15 min)
Step 5 Skincare Routine Guidance — Personalised advice on a simple daily skincare routine to maintain your results at home (5 min)
Total Duration Approximately 40 minutes
Suitable For All skin types, including sensitive skin
